Research Interests

  • Social Network Data Mining:

    Now we are working with the professors in Carnegie Mellon University, University of California Davis, and City University of Hong Kong to mining online social networks, such as Yelp, Apache and GitHub OSS Communities, StachExchange Q&A Websites, and so on.We try to use the methodologies in network science and machine learning to understand and predict online individual and collaborative human behaviors.

  • Deep Learning & Security:

    We try to use the methodologies in network science and optimization to design better deep learning frameworks, and then apply them in online social network data mining and computer vision in industry. Meanwhile, we also focus on the security problems in the appication of deep learning technologies.
